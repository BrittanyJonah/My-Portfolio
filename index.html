Services i offer to my clients

Services

Website Design

My approach to website design is to create a beautiful website that strengthens your company’s brand while ensuring ease of use and simplicity for your audience.

Web Development

The web development process involves taking the graphical elements defined in the design process and coding them into a custom made theme just for you.

Responsive Design

Having a responsive layout means that your website fluidly resizes for optimal viewing regardless of the screen size or device (e.g. iPhone, iPad).

Logo Design

My goal is to design a professional yet creative logo or company brand that reflects your company’s spirit and leaves a lasting, memorable effect.

SEO (Search Engine Optimisation)

SEO is the practice of improving and promoting a website in order to increase the number of visitors it receives.

Social Media Integration

Social media integration enables your website to post updates to Facebook, displays a Twitter feed, and links to your social media accounts.
